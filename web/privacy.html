SKOGARK

Privacy Policy — last updated July 23, 2026

The short version

SKOGARK collects no data. None. Not your name, not your location, not your gameplay, not your device identifiers. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking in the iOS app.

The iOS app

Everything happens on your device. Your game progress is stored locally and never leaves your phone or tablet. The optional Round-the-World Ticket purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store; we never see your payment details or purchase history beyond what is required to unlock content on your device.

The web version

The free web version at this site uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly counter, to tally page visits. It stores no cookies and collects no personal information. That's the only measurement anywhere in SKOGARK, and it applies to the website only — never the iOS app.
